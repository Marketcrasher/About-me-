My Career Goals

What I hope to do for a living after college

After completing my college education, I hope to work as a software developer in a FAANG company that values creativity, innovation, and social responsibility.

I am particularly interested in working on projects that have the potential to make a positive impact on society, such as developing applications for healthcare, education, or even people.

In addition, I also hope to continue learning and growing as a developer and contribute to open-source projects and communities!
